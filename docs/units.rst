=====
Units
=====

GRANAD uses the following unit conventions

.. list-table:: Units
   :widths: 50 50
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Quantity
     - Unit
   * - Energy
     - eV
   * - Distance
     - Ångström
   * - Time
     - :math:`0.659 \cdot 10^{-15}` s
   * - Charge
     - :math:`1.6 \cdot 10^{-19}` C
   * - Electric field strength
     - :math:`10^{10}` V/m
